Erin Moncrieff

Hey, what's up?

My name's Erin. I'm a very passionate graphic designer specializing in illustraion, event design, and typography. I would love to dabble in the arts of book design, and/or magazine design! I'm always looking to learn new things.

I'm originally from a small town outside of Montreal, but I'm currently living in good ole Gatineau. No, unfortunately I don't speak much french. But I do speak fluent franglais! I'm working on it. I'm also quite passionate about my pets, and animals in general, cats being #1 on the list. Some call it a problem, I also say it's a problem. A problem I don't plan on fixing!

Eastway Kennels Rebrand

This is a rebranding project I did for school. Eastway Kennels is a kennel I used to bring my dog to. Everytime I went I would cringe at their clip art logo, so I decided to redo it for them. The logo features the face of a cat on top of the face of a right facing dog.

Mandala
